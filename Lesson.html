Lessons Learned

FRC Team #5980

For Rookie Teams

Head Coach: Elizabeth Neubig
Team website:East Grand Rapids Robotics
Form of sponsor recognition: To recognize our sponsors we put them on our T-shirts, have a page on our website dedicated to them, and we have a poster in our pit with all of our sponsors. We keep them informed about how we are doing through email communication.


Lessons Learned and Student Quotes:

  1. "It's Not About The Robot"-James Ludwig mentor
  2. "Always wear safety glasses"-Maggie Benham '18
  3. "Don't eat pita bread and hold the robot"-Ben Strauss '16 dropped robot while holding it with one hand.
  4. "Don't stand over scissor lift face first while adjusting it"-Nathan Strodtbeck '19 had the scissor lift explode on him nearly hitting his head.
  5. "When you are sawing and hear an annoying noise, you attach a clamp further down the metal to lessen the noise"-Ben Strauss '16
  6. "Lift up the robot after putting in new code, so it doesn't run into a wall"-Anton Ludwig '19
  7. Have fun getting to know each other
  8. Have answers prepared for judges and anticipate questions
  9. Before matches talk with your alliance partners, make sure your battery is charged, and check your connections
